About

Founded by Ryan Athuda, the Blackjack Club at University of Ottawa teaches students the strategies, concepts, and fundamentals behind blackjack in a fun and social environment. Members can learn card game techniques, meet new people, and participate in annual casino trips. Meetings are held every Wednesday at 5:00 PM in FSS 2027.

Did you know? Blackjack is one of the only casino games where the player's decisions actually affect the outcome. We focus on basic strategy, bankroll management, and counting card theories (for educational purposes!).

Important Info

  1. Where: FSS 2027
  2. When: Fridays @5pm-8pm
  3. Experience Level: Players of all skill levels welcome!!
