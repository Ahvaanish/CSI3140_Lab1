About

Founded by Ryan Athuda, the Blackjack Club at University of Ottawa teaches students the strategies, concepts, and fundamentals behind blackjack in a fun and social environment. Members can learn card game techniques, meet new people, and participate in annual casino trips. Meetings are held every Wednesday at 5:00 PM in FSS 2027 It’s a great way to make friends, build confidence, and have fun with others who share similar interests.

Important Info

  1. Where: FSS 2027
  2. When: Fridays @5pm-8pm
  3. Experience Level: Players of all skill levels welcome!!
